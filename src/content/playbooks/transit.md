# Transit Playbook

## Vertical Overview
VerityAI™ SLiM for Transit — predictive intelligence for urban mobility: rail, bus, subway, light rail, and airport connectors. Turns the data transit agencies already generate into real-time operational advantage.

## The Core Problem
Transit agencies capture millions of data points daily (platform cameras, IoT sensors, ticket scans, crowd movement, operations logs) but still struggle to anticipate disruptions, prevent breakdowns, and deliver seamless journeys.

## Key Pain Points (Discovery)
- Maintenance issues caught too late → cascading delays and costly outages
- Safety cues on platforms and trains go unseen until incidents escalate
- Reactive operations reduce efficiency and erode public trust
- Disconnected systems prevent real-time rider engagement and optimization
- Dispatching is demand-blind: routes and schedules set without real-time demand data

## VAI Capabilities That Resonate

### Operations & Maintenance
Correlates sensor, telematics, and video data to forecast failures before they happen. Optimizes maintenance schedules and minimizes disruptions across fleets and networks.
**Impact: Reduce unplanned downtime by up to 30%, extend asset life.**

### Stations & Facilities
Monitors passenger flow, detects hazards and unsafe behavior (platform-edge risk, altercations, unattended items), triggers interventions in real time.
**Impact: Prevent incidents before they escalate, improve passenger safety and confidence.**

### Revenue Management & Protection
Identifies fare evasion patterns across subway, bus, and light rail using AI video analytics. Detects fare leakage and optimizes collections.
**Impact: Recover lost revenue, ensure equitable fare compliance.**

### Traffic & Mobility
Optimizes rail and bus dispatching with AI-driven demand forecasts and congestion predictions. Analyzes vehicle and pedestrian patterns to optimize multimodal networks.
**Impact: Improve on-time performance by 25%, enhance rider satisfaction.**

### AI Governance & Transparency
Every AI recommendation includes confidence scores, reasoning, and audit trails — ensuring accountability and regulatory compliance.
**Impact: Build public trust through transparent, explainable decision-making.**
