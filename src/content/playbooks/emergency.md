# Emergency Services Playbook

## Vertical Overview
VerityAI™ SLiM for Law Enforcement and District Attorneys — multimodal AI that turns fragmented evidence and live feeds into unified, actionable intelligence. Covers: police departments, sheriff's offices, DA offices, emergency coordination.

## The Core Problem
Public safety teams are stretched thin: officers buried in paperwork (nearly half their shift), dispatchers missing critical cues after just 20 minutes of nonstop monitoring, investigators waiting weeks for phone analysis, DA offices buried under evidence they can't process fast enough.

## Key Pain Points by Persona

### Law Enforcement (Patrol, Dispatch, Investigations)
- Dispatchers miss critical cues after 20 minutes of nonstop monitoring
- Officers spend nearly half their shift on reports instead of patrol
- Investigators wait weeks to review seized phones and hours of video
- Supervisors struggle to catch every alert when call volumes spike
- No real-time awareness of what's happening across jurisdictions

### District Attorneys (Prosecutors, Investigators)
- Critical evidence hidden in thousands of pages of police reports and hundreds of hours of video
- Charging decisions delayed while staff searches fragmented systems for corroborating evidence
- Weeks spent manually reviewing body cam footage, 911 calls, interviews, surveillance footage
- Inconsistencies and exculpatory details overlooked under time pressure and caseload

## VAI Capabilities — Law Enforcement

### Detect Threats Instantly
Monitors restricted zones, crowding, fights, unusual movement, and unattended items across holding cells, booking areas, and city cameras. Prioritizes alerts so dispatchers know exactly where to focus.
**Impact: Teams respond to the right threats at the right time, preventing escalation.**

### Investigate Phones & Digital Evidence in Minutes
Search texts, images, videos, and calls on seized phones instantly. Correlate multimodal evidence across suspects, communications, and locations.
**Impact: Replace weeks of manual review — faster charges, stronger cases.**

### Streamline Reporting & Optimize Patrols
Automatically draft reports from body-worn cameras and dispatch logs. Predictive deployment insights balance coverage and reduce officer fatigue.
**Impact: Free officers from up to 40% of admin work.**

### Explain Every Alert with Confidence
AI reasoning, confidence scores, and full audit trails for every alert. Supports supervisors, courts, and community transparency.
**Impact: Protects officers in legal proceedings, builds community trust.**

## VAI Capabilities — District Attorneys

### Unified Evidence Workspace
Brings police reports, PDFs, images, body-cam video, 911 audio, interview recordings, and surveillance footage into one environment.
**Impact: Stop jumping between systems. See everything that matters in one place.**

### Multimodal Correlation
Links people, locations, timestamps, vehicles, and events across different evidence types — strengthens narratives, spots contradictions, identifies missing pieces.
**Impact: Build stronger cases with complete visibility.**

### Advanced Search & Summarization
Surfaces relevant moments, quotes, and entities across thousands of pages and hours of media. AI-generated summaries accelerate understanding.
**Impact: Find the needle in the haystack in minutes, not days.**

### Automated Timelines & Narratives
Generates draft chronologies and case outlines that prosecutors refine — ready for charging decisions, motions, and trial strategy.
**Impact: Reduce case prep time by up to 60%.**

## Hardware & Deployment
- **Entry point**: NVIDIA DGX Spark — connects up to 10 data streams (body-worn cameras, surveillance feeds, dispatch audio, sensor data, case management systems, evidence repositories)
- **Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II, CJIS — purpose-built for law enforcement and legal requirements
- **Deployment**: Hours to actionable intelligence
- **Phase 1**: 3-month pilot, one precinct or one DA team
- **Phase 2**: Scale agency-wide or county-wide
