# Smart City Playbook

## Vertical Overview
City and campus intelligence — connecting existing cameras, sensors, and data feeds to give operators instant awareness of what's happening now, and investigators the ability to reconstruct any event after the fact.

## The Core Problem
Cities are data-rich but insight-poor. Incidents surface through phone calls, manual checks, or reports written hours later. Maintenance failures build through signals nobody connected in time. Investigation means manually scrubbing footage for days.

## Key Pain Points (Discovery)
- Time lag between incident and awareness — "finding out later instead of knowing now"
- False alarms waste crew time and erode trust in alerts
- Post-event investigation is manual, slow, fragmented
- No defensible accountability record when leadership or legal asks what happened
- Staff hours consumed writing reports instead of acting on intelligence

## VAI Capabilities That Resonate

### Real-Time Monitoring
Always watching, instantly alerting. When something significant happens, the right person is notified with footage, sensor data, and context attached. No screen-watching required.

### Investigation
Operators ask plain-language questions. VAI searches every camera, sensor log, and data source simultaneously — full picture assembled in minutes, not days.

### Automated Reporting
Incident summaries, shift briefings, and department reports generated automatically from actual data. Team reviews and approves; the work is already done.

### Accountability Record
Automatically documents what happened, what was detected, and what action was taken — structured record for leadership, legal, and public inquiries.

## Art of the Possible — Demo Scenarios
1. **Utility sensor spikes at 3am** — VAI confirms visually, distinguishes real leak from false alarm, notifies on-call crew with everything needed before they leave the house.
2. **Hit-and-run at a busy intersection** — Investigators type a description. VAI searches every camera across the network for the last 6 hours and returns a timestamped movement trail in minutes.
3. **Crowd building at a public venue** — VAI detects density patterns, compares to historical baselines, alerts supervisors early enough to reroute before a situation develops.
4. **Leadership asks what happened during last week's outage** — Complete incident timeline across every affected system, camera, and sensor — generated in one document. Ready for the briefing or legal team.
5. **Road closure ripples through transit network** — VAI detects the closure, correlates with bus delays across three routes, surfaces the connection before anyone connects the dots manually.
6. **New director asks "what's happening in my city right now?"** — They type the question. VAI answers — pulling from live feeds across infrastructure, transit, utilities, and public safety into a single plain-language briefing.
