# VerityAI — Product Profile

## Full Name
VerityAI™ (also referred to as VAI or VisionAI)

## Tagline
"What changes when your city can see everything in real time and investigate anything after."

## What It Is
VerityAI is an AI-powered city and campus intelligence platform built by Centific. It connects existing cameras, sensors, and data feeds — giving operators instant awareness of what's happening now, and investigators the ability to reconstruct any event after the fact.

It is the enterprise platform. SLiM is the entry product built on top of it.

## Core Capabilities

### Real-Time (Always Watching)
- Monitors every connected feed around the clock
- Delivers alerts the moment a sensor or camera detects an issue
- Alert includes: footage, sensor data, and context — not just a notification
- Nobody has to watch a screen for it to work

### Investigate (Ask Anything, Search Everything)
- After an event, operators ask plain-language questions
- Searches every camera, sensor log, and data source simultaneously
- No manual footage scrubbing, no cross-referencing spreadsheets
- Returns a complete, timestamped picture in minutes
