# VAI SLiM — Agent Profile

## Full Name
VAI SLiM (Start Small, Launch Immediately — or "Start Small, Scale Smart")

## Tagline
"Start Small, Scale Smart" / "Turning urban complexity into decisive action in hours, not months"

## What It Is
SLiM is the fastest path to VAI™ for any city, agency, or transit operator. It is the entry product built on the full VAI™ enterprise platform. Designed to eliminate the barriers that slow down AI adoption: infrastructure overhaul, long procurement cycles, and months-long deployments.

One box. Ten data streams. Insights in hours.

## Hardware
- **NVIDIA DGX Spark** — the compute engine (drop-in appliance)
- **NVIDIA DeepStream** — real-time video analytics pipeline
- **Triton™ Inference Server** — runs multimodal AI agents

## What It Connects (10 Data Streams)
Connect any combination of:
- Cameras (platform, vehicle, surveillance, body-worn)
- Sensors (IoT, environmental, traffic, infrastructure)
- Audio feeds (dispatch, 911, interviews)
- Ticketing / fare systems
- Case management and evidence repositories
- Operations and dispatch logs

| Phase | Description |

## Pick ONE Focus Area for Phase 1
Choosing a narrow pilot focus is the key to a fast win. Suggested focus areas by vertical:

**Smart City / General**
- Public safety monitoring (disturbance detection, crowd density)
- Infrastructure monitoring (sensor anomaly detection + camera confirmation)
- Automated reporting and shift briefings

**Transit**
- Predictive maintenance (vehicle sensors + telematics)
- Platform safety (crowd detection, unattended items, edge risk)

- Dispatching optimization (demand forecasting)

**Law Enforcement**
- Threat detection (surveillance + body-worn cameras)
- Digital forensics (seized phone evidence review)

**District Attorneys**
- Unified evidence workspace
- Case summarization and timelines
- Cross-case pattern analysis
